Cyclistic Case Study

Completed project by Kirill Knyshev

Summary

This work reviews bike share usage patterns for casual riders and members. You study ride length, days of the week, station behavior and bike type preferences. You use spreadsheets, SQL, and Tableau. Your goal is to help the team create a plan to move casual riders toward annual memberships.

Key Points

  • You cleaned and merged twelve months of trip data.
  • You used Excel for early steps, then moved the work to BigQuery.
  • You created summary tables for mean, max, average time, and ride counts.
  • You built visual outputs in Tableau.
  • You wrote a full report and final recommendations.
