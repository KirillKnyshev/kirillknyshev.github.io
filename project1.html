Case Study

Global Sales Analysis

Project Overview

A retail company needed to identify which regions had declining profit margins despite rising sales volume. The goal was to pinpoint inefficiencies in the logistics network and provide actionable insights for stakeholders.

* Visual representation of regional profit margin trends.

Tools & Technology

SQL: Data extraction and cleaning of 50,000+ records.
Python: Exploratory Data Analysis using Pandas and Seaborn.
Tableau: Final executive dashboard for stakeholders.

Key Insights & Results

  • Identified Loss: Found a 12% profit margin leak in the Midwest region due to inefficient shipping routes.
  • Optimization: Proposed new logistics logic that saves an estimated $15,000 monthly.
  • Automation: Created a recurring SQL script that updates the sales dashboard automatically every 24 hours.
